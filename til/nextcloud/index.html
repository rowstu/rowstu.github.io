Nextcloud autostart

Home / Browse/ Tilnextcloud

The Linux Nextcloud client now autostarts as part of the D-BUS Service. So even if you exit the client or kill the processes it restarts. To prevent this do the following:

mkdir -p ~/.local/share/dbus-1/services/
cat <<EOF > ~/.local/share/dbus-1/services/com.nextcloudgmbh.Nextcloud.service
[D-BUS Service]
Name=com.nextcloudgmbh.Nextcloud
Exec=/bin/false
EOF

This creates a D-BUS override. Remember to remove it when you want to have the client autostart through D-BUS.

Then kill the nextcloud processes with:

killall nextcloud

You can see for yourself here:

cat /usr/share/dbus-1/services/com.nextcloudgmbh.Nextcloud.service

[D-BUS Service]
Name=com.nextcloudgmbh.Nextcloud
Exec=/usr/bin/nextcloud --background

How to Calculate VAT

To add VAT:

  • Multiply the original price by (1 + VAT%/100)
  • Example: £100 × (1 + 20/100) = £120

To remove VAT:

  • Divide the total price by (1 + VAT%/100)
  • Example: £120 ÷ (1 + 20/100) = £100

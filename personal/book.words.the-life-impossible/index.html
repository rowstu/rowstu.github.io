words from The Life Impossible Book - by Matt Haig

anhedonia - the inability to experience joy or pleasure

finca - Spanish term for estate

misanthropic - having or showing a dislike of other people; unsociable.

chiaroscuro - Italian term which translates as light-dark, and refers to the balance and pattern of light and shade in a painting or drawing.

penumbra - a space of partial illumination (as in an eclipse) between the perfect shadow on all sides and the full light.

Notes on Suffering and Desire

The more you desire something, the more it slips from your grasp. the more you seek, the more you lose the way.

What to do, well, nothing, you can’t simply say, “stop desiring”. It just won’t work, in the same way that you cannot stop thinking about something by thinking about it. It’s a paradox, that exists because of thought.

So how do you achieve something? In simple terms, you don’t? By trying to achieve, you are pushing away. by relaxing and going with the flow of things is where the magic happens, being with the universe, saying yes, all of these things are going with the flow.

The key is to feel it.

Feel and sense the world as it would be should the achievement has happened, then forget about it.

Sense the flow, don’t resist the happening and non-happening.

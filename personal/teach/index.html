What to teach them about fear

Right now, your main job in life is to handle your fears and learn from the challenges you face every day. These challenges are things like school, gymnastics, and relationships with your friends and family.

Think of these things as your own hero’s quest.

When you feel afraid, what does that fear tell you about yourself? What can you learn from it?

Can you stay calm and let the fear pass, or does it take over and make you want to run away?

In the end, it’s up to you, the hero facing the mythical dragon, to face your fear and do the right thing, even if it’s hard. Doing the easy thing, like asking Mom and Dad to fix it, might feel good now, but it won’t help you grow. The next “dragon” will only seem bigger if you choose the easy way out.

What is inspiration

Inspiration comes from the union between heart and mind in the absence of importance.

Inspiration is the state of elation which makes the creative process easy, simple and most importantly, brilliant.

You are never truly inspired when you are working on something you do not feel passionate about.

Why is it that inspiration suddenly appears and then dissapears?

The truth is, it doesn’t dissapear. In fact, it doesn’t appear either. It is simply freed up when importance is dropped. Desire will not help you achieve your goals.

Inspiration will not arrive when you sit inactive, expectantly waiting for her to arrive.

Inspiration will not arrive until you release the state of anticipation.

Abandon the desire to achieve your goals. For if the goal is truly yours you will not suceed in getting rid of it for long anyway. Sooner or later the goal will be fulfilled. The will to have and the complete abscense of insistence and determination will play a key role in acheivement.

The key is to just start, inspiration is freed up in the act of working. By putting one foot in front of the other on a path which my not necassarily be THE path will free up inspiration to strike.

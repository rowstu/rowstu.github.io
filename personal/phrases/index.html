Privacy

Arguing that you don’t care about the right to privacy because you have nothing to hide is no different than saying you don’t care about free speech because you have nothing to say. – Edward Snowdon

Meme

An element of a culture or system of behaviour passed from one individual to another by immitation or other non-genetic means.

Comes from the greek, that which is imitated (MEMETIC).

Could be called a unit to carry cultural ideas, a way to represent an idea, symbol or practices.

How to answer the question, “How are you?”

I am well or I am good?

I am doing well ( My general condition is good)

I am good ( what I am doing is good)

Well applies to the verb doing and good applies to the unspoken noun.

Good is an adjective, well is an adverb.

Good modifies a noun, well modifies a verb.

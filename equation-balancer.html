Tips

Syntax

Use element symbols with numbers: H2O, CO2, Ca(OH)2. Separate compounds with + and sides with -> or = or →

Brackets

Parentheses are supported: Ca(OH)2 means 1 Ca, 2 O, 2 H. Nested brackets like Mg3(PO4)2 also work.

Balancing

The law of conservation of mass says atoms can't be created or destroyed — both sides must have equal atoms of each element.

Coefficients

Only coefficients (numbers in front) are changed when balancing. Subscripts inside formulas are never altered.

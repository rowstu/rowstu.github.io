Notes from the Book Jim and the Universe

have faith that the universe knows what it is doing and it is so. Don’t doubt, doubt wil kill it. Live your life like it is so. Feel happy about it. Feeling is everything.

Be grateful, say thank you to the universe all the time. Say it with sincerity and dont say it until you can feeeeeel the sincerity.

trust the universe. you don’t know, the universe knows.

show “active faith”, things don’t just turn up on your door step, you have to actively work in partnership with the universe. so if your intuition is telling you to do something, do it, you have to uphold your part of the bargain.

view everything as a success, in other words look on the bright side of things. everything happens for a reason and you’re not aware of that reason until after.

don’t rush anything, you don’t do anything until it feels right.

the universe is everything, it is space and stuff but it is also these chairs, the flowers, the people. All is one. When we ask for things, we do it because it wants us to. It wants us to work with it because it is an essential part of our being. People are unhappy because they think they are all alone, that they might lose it all at any moment. When the truth is the Universe will give it all to them if they only believed that is would. The truth is that nothing is truly lost because we are all alone.

dear universe, i give thanks that these feelings of doubt and fear disappear.

dear universe, thank you for the strength and faith you have provided me in order for me to follow the plan.

dear universe, let me follow your plan for me this day

no appologies and no guilt

don’t judge anyone, don’t resent them, just love them. view your relationship with everybody as a success.

help people if you can, but always respect their wishes if they do not wish to be helped

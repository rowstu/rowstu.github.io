Notes on the book The Happiness Advantage by Shawn Achor

The book states that happiness is not simply a result of success, but rather it is the cause of success.

Cultivating a positive mindset and outlook can lead to better health, stronger relationships, and greater success in all areas of life.

Seven principles that contribute to the happiness advantage:

  • The Happiness Advantage - happiness leads to success, not the other way around.
  • The Fulcrum and the Lever - changing our mindset can help us leverage our potential for success.
  • The Tetris Effect - our brains can become patterned to see either positive or negative patterns, and we can train our brains to see the positive.
  • Falling Up - we can use adversity as an opportunity for growth.
  • The Zorro Circle - starting with small, manageable goals can help us gain control over bigger challenges.
  • The 20-Second Rule - making small changes that take 20 seconds or less can have a big impact on our habits and productivity.
  • Social Investment - connecting with others and investing in relationships can lead to greater happiness and success.

Happiness is not just a byproduct of success, but it is a key driver of it.

Rules
  • Addition: Symbols are placed from left to right in order of value, starting with the largest. Add their values together.
  • Subtraction: When a smaller value appears before a larger value, subtract the smaller from the larger.
  • Repetition: A symbol cannot be repeated more than three times in succession (I, X, C, M only).
  • Range: Standard Roman numerals can represent numbers from 1 to 3999.

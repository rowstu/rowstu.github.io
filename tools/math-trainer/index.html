Mental Math Tutorials

Master these techniques to calculate faster in your head and on paper.

üî¢ Addition Tricks

Making Tens (Complements)

Look for pairs that make 10, then add the rest.

7 + 8 + 3 + 2 ‚Üí (7 + 3) + (8 + 2) = 10 + 10 = 20

Left-to-Right Addition

Add from left to right instead of right to left ‚Äî it's more natural for mental math.

47 + 36 ‚Üí 40 + 30 = 70, then 7 + 6 = 13 ‚Üí 83

Round and Adjust

Round to an easy number, calculate, then adjust back.

67 + 29 ‚Üí 67 + 30 = 97, minus 1 = 96

‚ûñ Subtraction Tricks

Counting Up (Shopkeeper's Method)

Instead of subtracting, count up from the smaller number.

83 - 47 ‚Üí 47 ‚Üí 50 (3) ‚Üí 80 (30) ‚Üí 83 (3) = 36

Round and Adjust

Round the number you're subtracting, then compensate.

92 - 37 ‚Üí 92 - 40 = 52, plus 3 = 55

Same Change Method

Add or subtract the same amount from both numbers to make easier calculations.

84 - 39 ‚Üí 85 - 40 = 45

‚úñÔ∏è Multiplication Tricks

Multiplying by 11

For 2-digit numbers: add the digits and put the sum in the middle.

36 √ó 11 ‚Üí 3 _ 6, insert 3+6=9 ‚Üí 396

If the sum is 10+, carry the 1: 57 √ó 11 = 5_(12)_7 = 627

Multiplying by 5

Multiply by 10, then halve the result.

48 √ó 5 ‚Üí 48 √ó 10 = 480, halve = 240

Multiplying by 9

Multiply by 10, then subtract the original number.

23 √ó 9 ‚Üí 23 √ó 10 = 230, minus 23 = 207

Doubling and Halving

Make one number easier by doubling it and halving the other.

16 √ó 25 ‚Üí 8 √ó 50 = 4 √ó 100 = 400

Squaring Numbers Ending in 5

Take the tens digit, multiply by (itself + 1), append 25.

35¬≤ ‚Üí 3 √ó 4 = 12, append 25 ‚Üí 1225

Breaking Apart (Distribution)

Split one number into parts, multiply separately, add results.

7 √ó 23 ‚Üí 7 √ó 20 = 140, 7 √ó 3 = 21 ‚Üí 161

‚ûó Division Tricks

Divisibility Rules

√∑2: Last digit is even
√∑3: Digit sum divisible by 3
√∑4: Last 2 digits √∑ 4
√∑5: Ends in 0 or 5
√∑6: Divisible by both 2 and 3
√∑9: Digit sum divisible by 9
√∑10: Ends in 0
√∑11: Alternating sum = 0 or √∑11

Dividing by 5

Double the number, then divide by 10.

135 √∑ 5 ‚Üí 135 √ó 2 = 270, √∑ 10 = 27

Halving Repeatedly

To divide by 4, halve twice. To divide by 8, halve three times.

96 √∑ 8 ‚Üí 96 ‚Üí 48 ‚Üí 24 ‚Üí 12

Factor and Conquer

Break the divisor into factors and divide step by step.

180 √∑ 12 ‚Üí 180 √∑ 3 = 60, √∑ 4 = 15
